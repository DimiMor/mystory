Who am I?

My curiosity and will for improvement have made me someone who loves to innovate. Putting this all together, I worked more than 10 years as a Business Analyst interpreting and managing demands from business and technical areas. Since I can't get stuck in only one thing, I'm currently in love with the development and web programming, and dedicating my time to learn and expand my knowledge in tools such as HTML, CSS, JS, React, etc.
Lets see where this will take me...
